# 🖋️ Ajinkya Novel Helper

[![Live Demo](https://img.shields.io/badge/Live_Website-Ajinkya_Novel_Helper-blue?style=for-the-badge&logo=googlechrome)](https://ajinkya-novel-helper.web.app/)

**Ajinkya Novel Helper** is a powerful, AI-integrated workspace engineered specifically for authors, world-builders, and storytellers. It bridges the gap between creative writing and advanced data management, providing a unified platform to draft chapters, construct complex lore, and collaborate with readers—all supercharged by Google's Gemini AI.

---

## ✨ The Gemini AI Powerhouse
At the core of the application is a deeply integrated AI engine, powered by user-provided **Gemini Custom API Keys**. This ensures unparalleled privacy, zero rate-limiting from the app side, and tailored AI responses.

* **AI Command Center & Expert Assistant:** A dedicated portal to consult with a context-aware AI on plot holes, character development, and narrative pacing.
* **AI Auto Re-Writer & Grammar Checker:** Instantly polish prose, fix grammatical errors, or completely rewrite paragraphs to fit a specific tone.
* **Suggestion AI:** Get real-time, context-driven suggestions to overcome writer's block and keep the story flowing.
* **Novel-to-Movie Script Converter:** Automatically format and adapt your prose chapters into professional screenplay/movie script formats with a single click.

---

## 🌍 Deep World-Building Engine
Never lose track of your universe. The app features a relational database structure designed specifically for complex storytelling.

* **Lore & Regions:** Document magical systems, historical events, and geographical locations.
* **Character Profiles:** Track character arcs, motivations, relationships, and physical descriptions.
* **Timeline & Arc Feature:** Visually map out the chronological progression of your narrative and structure multi-chapter story arcs with precision.
* **Universal Search Feature:** Instantly locate any character, item, or lore entry across your entire workspace.

---

## 📖 Writing Dashboard & Productivity
Designed to keep authors focused and motivated during the drafting process.

* **Chapter Management:** Organize, draft, and rearrange chapters seamlessly.
* **Daily Word Counter & Progress Bar:** Set daily goals, track your writing velocity, and visualize your manuscript's overall progress.
* **Recycle Bin:** Safely manage deleted chapters, lore, or characters with a robust recovery system to prevent accidental data loss.

---

## 🤝 Beta Reader Collaboration
Streamline the feedback loop without exposing your entire backend workspace.

* **Private Reader Links:** Generate secure, read-only links to specific chapters or complete manuscripts to share with beta readers.
* **Inline Comments:** Beta readers can leave direct feedback and comments on the shared text, allowing authors to review critiques directly within their workspace.

---

## 🔒 Security & Data Management
Built on top of a robust **Firebase** architecture, ensuring your intellectual property is safe, secure, and accessible anywhere.

* **Firebase Authentication:** Secure, encrypted user login and session management.
* **Cloud Storage & Real-Time Database:** All manuscript data, lore entries, and user settings are instantly synced to the cloud using Firebase's secure database rules.
* **Import / Export Functionality:** Maintain absolute control over your work. Export your entire novel and world-building data for local backups, or import existing manuscripts to seamlessly continue your work.

---

## 🚀 Getting Started

### Prerequisites
* A modern web browser.
* A valid Google Gemini API Key (for AI features).

### Accessing the App
Simply navigate to the live application: **[Ajinkya Novel Helper](https://ajinkya-novel-helper.web.app/)**

1. Create a secure account using the Firebase authentication portal.
2. Navigate to Settings to securely input your **Gemini API Key**.
3. Create a new Novel project and start building your world!
